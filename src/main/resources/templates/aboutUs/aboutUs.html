About Us

Established in New York City over fifteen years ago, SneakersClub revolutionized sneaker retail as the original consignment store for rare shoes. Carrying the latest drops, coveted classics and hard-to-find grails, SneakersClub has evolved from a one-stop sneaker destination to a cultural hub for sneaker enthusiasts and novices alike. With three brick-and-mortar locations in London, Los Angeles and Buenos Aires, SneakersClub remains the premier source for authentic, rare shoes. SneakersClub is owned and operated by GOAT Group. To learn more about SneakersClub's parent company, please visit the official GOAT Group website.

At our store, we are unwavering in our commitment to providing our customers with sneakers of the highest quality. We understand that when you invest in a pair of sneakers, you expect them to not only look great but also offer superior comfort, durability, and performance. That's why we go to great lengths to ensure that every sneaker in our inventory meets and exceeds the highest standards of quality. The quality of our sneakers starts with the materials used in their construction. We prioritize sourcing premium materials that are not only durable but also provide optimal comfort and support. From high-quality leathers and suede to breathable mesh fabrics and cutting-edge synthetic materials, we select materials that are built to last and offer superior performance. We understand that the choice of materials greatly impacts the overall quality and feel of the sneakers, and we are committed to delivering nothing but the best. To ensure that every pair of sneakers meets our stringent quality criteria, we have implemented a rigorous quality control process. Each pair undergoes thorough inspections at various stages of production, from the initial cutting and stitching to the final assembly and finishing touches. Our dedicated quality control team meticulously checks every detail, ensuring that the sneakers meet our strict standards for stitching integrity, construction, adhesive strength, and overall finish.

At our store, we take great pride in our team of dedicated and knowledgeable staff members who are passionate about sneakers and committed to delivering exceptional customer service. We believe that the expertise and guidance of our staff are invaluable in helping you find the perfect pair of sneakers. Our team is comprised of true sneaker enthusiasts who live and breathe the sneaker culture. They stay up to date with the latest trends, releases, and collaborations, constantly expanding their knowledge and understanding of the industry. Their deep-rooted passion for sneakers allows them to provide insights and recommendations that go beyond just selling a product. When you visit our store, you'll be greeted by friendly and approachable staff members who are genuinely excited to assist you. They understand that choosing the right pair of sneakers is a personal decision, and they take the time to listen to your preferences, lifestyle, and intended use for the sneakers. Whether you're a seasoned sneakerhead looking for a specific limited edition release or a casual shopper in need of guidance, our staff is dedicated to understanding your needs and preferences to ensure a tailored shopping experience.
